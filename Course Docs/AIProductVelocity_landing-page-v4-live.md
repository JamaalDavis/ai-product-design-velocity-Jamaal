## 4. WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

### Section headline:
> Build a complete product with AI: From finding the right opportunity to an on-brand product to defending your decisions under real pressure.

### Outcome 1: Find high-impact opportunities backed by evidence, not guesswork
- Use AI to surface patterns in company data and market signals that humans miss.
- Build a reusable discovery system. Run it on any company's data to generate validated product hypotheses.
- Turn raw data into specific, defensible opportunities you can pitch with confidence.

### Outcome 2: Turn opportunities into product requirements tied to business value
- Use AI to draft product requirement documents that connect user problems to revenue and retention outcomes.
- Structure your thinking so product, design, and engineering can align on what to build and why.

### Outcome 3: Build on-brand prototypes that aligns Design System, not AI-generic
- Connect AI prototyping tools to your design system. Every screen matches your brand, not a template library.
- Go from validated hypothesis to working screens in hours, not weeks.
- Spot and fix where AI output breaks brand fidelity. Ship work with taste.

### Outcome 4: Sell your product decisions with a narrative that earns buy-in
- Assemble evidence, design rationale, and business impact into a story that survives hard questions
- Practice defending your reasoning live. Get challenged by instructors before the real room.
- Master "why this, why now" framing. The language that gets initiatives funded.

### Outcome 5: Build scalable AI workflows with minimal tools and zero noise
- Master a lean AI toolset that covers discovery, prototyping, and presentation. No tool overload.
- Create prompt libraries and workflow files you reuse across every project. Never start from scratch.
- Know where AI accelerates and where your judgment is the difference between slop and real product thinking.

## 10. FAQ

**What happens if I can't make a live session?**
All sessions are recorded and posted to your portal. You'll still access every material, file, and community discussion. That said, the live sessions are where real building and instructor feedback happen — we recommend attending live whenever possible.

**I work full-time, what is the expected time commitment?**
5-8 hours per week. Most students work full time and is able to commit to the deliverable work.

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No coding required. You need the willingness to open VS Code and follow along. AI tools handle the technical execution — your job is the thinking: what to build, why it matters, and how to defend it. That's the hard part. The tools are the easy part.

**How much time should I expect to spend per week?**
Plan for 5–8 hours per week: one 90-minute live session, 1–2 hours for async lessons and tool setup, and 1–2 hours building your capstone project. Designed for full-time professionals. Async content is lean — only what you need to build, nothing extra.

**What makes this different from other AI prototyping or "vibe coding" courses?**
Most courses teach you to build fast. We teach you to build right. The EIM framework grounds every prototype in evidence and business impact. You also learn to defend decisions and connect design to revenue — the part that gets projects funded. Speed without strategy is expensive guessing.

**What tools do I need and what's the cost beyond the course fee?**
Claude Pro is the only paid tool required. Other tools are free.

**Is this for designers, product managers, founders — or all three?**
All three. The roles are converging — PMs who can prototype, designers who can defend business impact, and founders who need to validate fast are all building the same muscle. You'll work with shared frameworks regardless of title. This course is where those skills meet.

**What do I walk away with at the end of the course?**
A working discovery system, an on-brand prototype, a defended product narrative, and a full library of prompts and workflow files. Every deliverable is reusable — run it on your own company's product the week you finish. Plus a Demo Day recording for your portfolio.

**What's the refund policy?**
This course is backed by the Maven Guarantee. You're eligible for a full refund before half point through the course. See Maven's satisfaction guarantee for full details.

**Who is this course NOT for?**
This isn't for people looking to learn AI production tools like Cursor or Lovable.
It's not an intro to design or product management.
This is for product builders who want to connect what they make to why it matters for the business, and prove it under pressure.
